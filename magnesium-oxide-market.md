<p><p>The magnesium oxide market comprises several types, including Dead-Burned Magnesium Oxide (DBM), Fused Magnesium Oxide (FM), Caustic Calcined Magnesium Oxide (CCM), and Synthetic Magnesium Oxide. DBM is produced through high-temperature roasting and is used in refractory applications. FM is generated by melting magnesium oxide and is highly pure, ideal for chemical applications. CCM is derived from calcining magnesium hydroxide and is utilized in various industrial applications. Synthetic Magnesium Oxide, manufactured through chemical processes, offers versatility across multiple sectors.</p></p>

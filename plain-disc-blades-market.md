<p><p>The plain disc blades market consists of two primary types: flat and concave disc blades. Flat disc blades are typically used for shallow tillage operations, offering even soil coverage and minimal soil disturbance. In contrast, concave disc blades are designed for deeper penetration and effective soil cutting, making them ideal for aggressive tilling and heavy-duty applications. Both types are essential in agriculture for various soil management practices, contributing to improved crop yields and sustainable farming.</p></p>

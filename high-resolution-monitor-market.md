<p><ul><li>1280x720 (720p)</li><li>1920x1080 (1080i)</li><li>1920x1080 progressive (1080p)</li><li>2560x1440</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-resolution monitor market comprises various display types, each defined by pixel dimensions. The 1280x720 (720p) resolution offers basic HD quality, suitable for casual use. The 1920x1080 (1080i) provides full HD but displays images in interlaced format, which can cause motion blur. In contrast, the 1920x1080 progressive (1080p) resolution delivers a smoother, clearer image ideal for gaming and video. Finally, the 2560x1440 resolution offers a superior QHD experience, enhancing detail and clarity for professional and gaming applications.</p></p>

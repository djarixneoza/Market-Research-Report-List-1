<p><p>The Crosslinked Polyethylene (PEX) tube market is categorized into three main types: PE-Xa, PE-Xb, and PE-Xc. PE-Xa is produced using a peroxide method, offering superior flexibility and temperature resistance. PE-Xb, made through a silane process, features moderate flexibility and is commonly used in plumbing systems. PE-Xc is crosslinked via chemical means, providing excellent chemical resistance and stability but is generally less flexible. Each type caters to specific applications in plumbing, heating, and industrial sectors, reflecting varying performance characteristics.</p></p>

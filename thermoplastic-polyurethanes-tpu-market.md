<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyurethanes (TPU) are versatile polymers known for their elasticity, durability, and chemical resistance. The TPU market is primarily segmented into three types: </p><p>1. **Polyester-based TPU** offers high strength and is suitable for applications requiring abrasion resistance.</p><p>2. **Polyether-based TPU** provides excellent hydrolytic stability, making it ideal for wet environments.</p><p>3. **Polycaprolactone-based TPU** features superior flexibility and a lower melting point, making it suitable for applications needing low-temperature processing and high elasticity. Each type caters to specific performance needs across various industries.</p></p>

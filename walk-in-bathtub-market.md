<p><p>The walk-in bathtub market is segmented into two main types: embedded and independent bathtubs. Embedded bathtubs are installed as part of the home’s existing structure, often requiring significant renovations. They offer seamless integration with the bathroom design. Independent bathtubs, however, are freestanding units that can be placed anywhere in the bathroom without extensive modifications. They provide flexibility in design and installation while catering to users seeking safety and accessibility, particularly for the elderly and those with mobility challenges.</p></p>

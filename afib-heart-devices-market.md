<p><p>The Afib heart devices market includes key types such as catheter ablation devices and maze surgery devices. Catheter ablation devices are used to destroy small areas of heart tissue causing abnormal electrical signals, effectively restoring normal rhythm. Maze surgery devices, on the other hand, involve creating scar tissue in a specific pattern within the heart to block erratic electrical impulses. Both approaches aim to improve patient outcomes by reducing atrial fibrillation episodes and enhancing overall cardiac function.</p></p>

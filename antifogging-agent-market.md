<p><strong>The Antifogging Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Outside Antifoggant</li><li>Within Antifoggant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antifogging agent market is categorized into two main types: outside antifogging agents and within antifogging agents. Outside antifogging agents are applied directly to the surface of materials, creating a protective layer that prevents condensation. In contrast, within antifogging agents are integrated into the material itself during manufacturing, providing long-lasting fog resistance. Both types are commonly used in applications such as eyewear, safety goggles, and automotive windshields, enhancing visibility and safety in various environments.</p></p>

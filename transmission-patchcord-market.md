<p><ul><li>Single Mode Fiber Patchcord</li><li>Multimode Fiber Patchcord</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transmission patchcord market is primarily divided into two types: single mode fiber patchcords and multimode fiber patchcords. Single mode patchcords utilize a single light path for longer distance communication, typically used in telecom and data center applications. In contrast, multimode patchcords support multiple light paths, making them suitable for short-distance applications like local area networks (LANs). Both types are essential for high-speed data transmission and are utilized in various networking and telecommunication infrastructures.</p></p>

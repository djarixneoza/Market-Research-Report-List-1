<p><p>Three-phase voltage monitoring relays are essential in managing electrical systems by detecting voltage abnormalities. The over voltage relay protects equipment from high voltage spikes, preventing damage by disconnecting supply when voltage exceeds a set threshold. Conversely, the under voltage relay safeguards against low voltage conditions, which can cause overheating or inefficient operation, by ensuring the system is disabled when voltage dips below a specified level. Together, they enhance system reliability and prolong equipment lifespan in industrial applications.</p></p>

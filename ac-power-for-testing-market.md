<p><p>AC power for testing encompasses various market types, mainly linear AC power sources and PWM (Pulse Width Modulation) AC power sources. Linear AC power sources provide stable and pure sine wave output, ideal for testing sensitive electronic devices, ensuring accurate performance evaluations. In contrast, PWM AC power sources generate AC signals by rapidly switching on and off, allowing greater control over output waveforms and efficiency. Both types are crucial for telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics testing, supporting diverse industry needs.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Passive Hydroponic Drip Systems</li><li>Active Hydroponic Drip Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydroponic drip systems are categorized into passive and active types. Passive hydroponic drip systems rely on gravity and capillary action to deliver nutrient solutions to plants, requiring minimal setup and maintenance. In contrast, active hydroponic drip systems employ pumps to distribute nutrients, offering greater control over water and nutrient delivery but requiring more complex infrastructure. Both systems enable efficient nutrient absorption and water conservation, appealing to various growers seeking sustainable and productive agricultural practices in controlled environments.</p></p>

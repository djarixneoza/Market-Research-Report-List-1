<p><ul><li>Resistance Temperature Detector</li><li>Thermistor</li><li>Thermocouple</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The temperature transmitter market encompasses various types, primarily including Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs), thermistors, and thermocouples. RTDs offer high accuracy and stability for industrial applications, operating based on the resistance change of metals with temperature. Thermistors, known for their sensitivity, have resistance that varies significantly with temperature and are ideal for precise measurements in small ranges. Thermocouples function by generating voltage through the junction of two different metals, providing a wide temperature range and quick response, making them suitable for diverse applications.</p></p>

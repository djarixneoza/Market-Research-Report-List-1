<p><p>Electric bidet seats can be categorized into three market types: tank, tankless, and hybrid. </p><p>Tank bidet seats store water in a reservoir, providing a consistent water supply but may have a limited capacity. Tankless models heat water on demand, offering unlimited warm water without storage limitations, ideal for smaller spaces. Hybrid versions combine features of both, maintaining a limited tank while also providing instant heating capabilities, ensuring efficiency and comfort. Each type caters to different user preferences and bathroom setups.</p></p>

<p><p>The holographic diffraction grating market is categorized into two main types: plane type and concave type holographic gratings. Plane type gratings are flat surfaces that diffract light in a predictable manner, commonly used in spectroscopy and optical systems. Concave type gratings, on the other hand, feature a curved surface that enhances resolution by focusing light, making them suitable for high-resolution applications in lasers and telescopes. Both types play vital roles in various scientific and industrial applications.</p></p>

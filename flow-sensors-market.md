<p><p>The flow sensors market includes several types catering to different applications. Turbine flow sensors measure fluid velocity through the rotation of blades, making them ideal for clean liquids. Electromagnetic flow sensors utilize electromagnetic fields and are effective for conductive fluids, especially in wastewater management. Thermal mass flow sensors gauge gas flow by measuring temperature changes, suitable for precise readings. Vortex flow sensors detect flow through vortices created by obstacles, suitable for various fluids. Other market types encompass innovative and specialized flow measurement technologies.</p></p>

<p><p>The metal bond blade market is segmented into SD grit type, SDC grit type, and CBN grit type. SD grit types are designed for general grinding applications, offering versatility and durability. SDC grit types incorporate a hybrid structure for enhanced performance and longer life, making them suitable for demanding tasks. CBN grit types utilize cubic boron nitride, ideal for cutting hard materials, providing superior precision and heat resistance. Each type serves specific industrial needs, catering to a range of manufacturing applications.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Artificial Retinal Implants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Epiretinal Implants</li><li>Subretinal Implants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The artificial retinal implants market primarily consists of two categories: epiretinal implants and subretinal implants. </p><p>Epiretinal implants are placed on the surface of the retina and stimulate retinal neurons directly, often benefiting individuals with diseases like age-related macular degeneration and retinitis pigmentosa. </p><p>Subretinal implants, on the other hand, are inserted beneath the retina, targeting photoreceptor cells to restore vision. Both types aim to improve visual perception in patients with severe retinal degeneration, advancing treatment options for vision loss.</p></p>

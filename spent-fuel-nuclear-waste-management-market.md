<p><p>The spent fuel and nuclear waste management market encompasses various waste categories based on their radioactivity levels. Low-level waste (LLW) includes items like protective clothing and tools, typically requiring minimal shielding. Intermediate-level waste (ILW) contains higher radioactivity and may require shielding during handling and storage. High-level waste (HLW), primarily from spent nuclear fuel, is highly radioactive and necessitates deep geological disposal due to its long-lived isotopes and intense heat. Each waste type demands specific management strategies to ensure safety and environmental protection.</p></p>

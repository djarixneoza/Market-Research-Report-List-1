In contrast, magnetic rowing machines employ magnetic resistance to provide a smoother and quieter operation, allowing for consistent resistance across various settings. Both types cater to different preferences in terms of feel, sound, and workout experience, appealing to diverse fitness enthusiasts.</p></p>

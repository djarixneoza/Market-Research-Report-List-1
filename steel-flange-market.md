<p><strong>The Steel Flange Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Weld Flange</li><li>Blind Flange</li><li>Slip-On Flange</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel flange market comprises several types, including weld flanges, blind flanges, and slip-on flanges. Weld flanges are designed for strong, permanent connections between pipes by welding. Blind flanges are used to seal the ends of piping systems, preventing the flow of fluids. Slip-on flanges are easily fitted over pipes and welded in place, allowing for quick installation. Other types may include threaded flanges and lap joint flanges, catering to various industrial applications and connection requirements.</p></p>

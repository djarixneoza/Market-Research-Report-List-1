<p><p>Soil fertility testing encompasses three main market types: physical, chemical, and biological testing. Physical testing evaluates soil structure, texture, and moisture retention, essential for understanding water movement and root growth. Chemical testing analyzes nutrient content, pH levels, and the presence of contaminants, directly influencing crop health and yield. Biological testing examines microbial activity and organic matter, reflecting soil health and ecosystem balance. Together, these testing types provide comprehensive insights for sustainable agricultural practices and effective soil management.</p></p>

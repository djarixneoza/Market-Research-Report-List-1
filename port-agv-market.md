<p><p>The Port Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV) market is primarily divided into two types: Vertical Transport AGVs and Horizontal Transport AGVs. Vertical Transport AGVs specialize in moving cargo vertically, typically used in stacking and retrieving containers in storage facilities. In contrast, Horizontal Transport AGVs are designed for moving goods horizontally across port terminals, facilitating the transfer between loading and unloading areas. Both types enhance operational efficiency, reduce labor costs, and improve safety in busy port environments by automating material handling.</p></p>

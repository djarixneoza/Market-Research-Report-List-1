<p><p>The aluminum extrusion market is categorized into three main types: mill finished, anodized, and powder-coated. Mill finished aluminum has a natural surface without added treatment, offering a raw aesthetic suited for various applications. Anodized aluminum undergoes an electrochemical process that enhances corrosion resistance and durability while providing a colorful, protective finish. Powder-coated aluminum is coated with a dry paint that is cured under heat, resulting in a tough, vibrant finish, ideal for both aesthetic appeal and protection against the elements.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Hot</li><li>Cold</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polypectomy forceps are specialized medical instruments used for the removal of polyps, primarily in gastrointestinal procedures. The market for these devices can be categorized into two types: hot and cold. Hot polypectomy forceps use electrosurgical energy to cauterize tissue during polyp removal, offering minimal bleeding and easier extraction. Conversely, cold polypectomy forceps operate without heat, relying on cutting edges to remove polyps while preserving surrounding tissue. Each type serves distinct clinical needs, influencing their adoption in medical practice.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Single Pulse</li><li>Multipulse</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pulsed laser power supply market is categorized into two main types: single pulse and multipulse systems. Single pulse power supplies generate one laser pulse per activation, ideal for applications requiring high peak power in short durations, such as material processing and medical procedures. In contrast, multipulse systems produce multiple pulses in quick succession, allowing for enhanced precision and control in tasks like surface treatment and intricate machining. Each type offers distinct benefits suited to varying industrial and research applications.</p></p>

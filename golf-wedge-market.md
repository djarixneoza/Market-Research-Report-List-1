<p><p>The golf wedge market consists of four primary types: pitching wedges, gap wedges, sand wedges, and lob wedges. Pitching wedges are designed for mid-range shots, providing accuracy and distance control. Gap wedges fill the distance gap between pitching and sand wedges, offering versatility for various approach shots. Sand wedges excel in bunker play and short chips around the green, featuring a wide sole for better performance in soft conditions. Lob wedges are designed for high, short shots, allowing golfers to clear obstacles with precision.</p></p>

<p><p>The adjuvants market comprises several types essential for enhancing vaccine efficacy. Adjuvant emulsions, such as oil-in-water formulations, improve antigen presentation and immune response. Pathogen components, derived from microbial sources, stimulate the immune system directly. Particulate adjuvants, including nanoparticles, facilitate targeted delivery and prolonged immune activation. Combination adjuvants combine multiple mechanisms to optimize immune reactions, offering tailored solutions for various vaccines. Each type plays a crucial role in advancing immunization strategies and improving public health outcomes.</p></p>

<p><p>The water-washed bentonite clay market is categorized into three primary types: sodium bentonite, calcium bentonite, and potassium bentonite. Sodium bentonite is highly absorbent and widely used in drilling fluids and pet litter. Calcium bentonite, known for its swelling properties, finds applications in cosmetics and pharmaceuticals. Potassium bentonite has excellent bonding capabilities, often used in fertilizers and soil conditioners. Each type serves distinct industrial applications, driven by their unique physicochemical properties and functionalities.</p></p>

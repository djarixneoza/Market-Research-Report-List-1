<p><p>The Building Design Software market is primarily categorized into two types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted online, providing accessibility from any device with internet connectivity, facilitating collaboration and automatic updates. This model typically operates on a subscription basis, reducing initial costs and allowing for scalability and flexibility in usage.</p></p>

<p><p>The Mid-Infrared Ultrafast Fiber Laser Market is segmented into two main types: Continuous Wave (CW) and Pulse lasers. CW lasers emit a constant beam of light, suitable for applications requiring steady energy output and high precision, such as materials processing and sensing. In contrast, Pulse lasers deliver energy in short bursts, allowing for ultrafast processing and high peak power, making them ideal for applications like medical diagnostics, telecommunications, and advanced scientific research. Each type caters to specific industrial and research needs.</p></p>

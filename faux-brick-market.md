<p><ul><li>Dry Pressed Brick</li><li>Extruded Brick</li><li>Malleable Brick</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The faux brick market comprises three main types: dry pressed brick, extruded brick, and malleable brick. Dry pressed brick is crafted by pressing clay into molds, resulting in a dense, durable product ideal for applications where strength is vital. Extruded brick is formed by forcing clay through a die, creating long sections that are then cut, suited for uniformity in large projects. Malleable brick, on the other hand, is flexible and can be shaped easily, making it popular for decorative and artistic applications.</p></p>

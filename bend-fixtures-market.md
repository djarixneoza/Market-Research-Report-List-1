<p><strong>The Bend Fixtures Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3-point Bend</li><li>4-point Bend</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bend fixtures are essential tools used in material testing to evaluate the flexural properties of materials. In the 3-point bend market, specimens are supported at two ends while a load is applied at the center, ideal for measuring the material's flexural strength. Conversely, the 4-point bend market involves supporting the specimen at two points and applying loads at two other points, providing a more uniform stress distribution. Both methods are crucial for assessing material durability and performance in various applications.</p></p>

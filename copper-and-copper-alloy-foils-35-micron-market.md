<p><p>The Copper and Copper-alloy Foils market, particularly those ≤35 microns, comprises two main types: Electrodeposition Copper Foil and Rolling Copper Foil. Electrodeposition involves depositing copper onto a substrate, allowing for precise thickness and uniformity, making it ideal for applications like printed circuit boards. In contrast, Rolling Copper Foil is produced through mechanical processes, resulting in thicker, stronger foils suitable for applications such as connectors and electrical components. Both types offer unique benefits catering to various industrial demands.</p></p>

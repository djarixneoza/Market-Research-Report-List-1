<p><p>The Roof Solar Mounting System market is categorized into flat roofs and pitched roofs. Pitched roof systems, often found in residential settings, use rack-mounted solutions to adjust angles for optimal sunlight capture. Each type caters to specific architectural designs and energy needs, enhancing solar energy harnessing while ensuring structural integrity and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>

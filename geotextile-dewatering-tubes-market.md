<p><p>The geotextile dewatering tubes market is categorized into two main types: woven and nonwoven geotextile dewatering tubes. Woven geotextile tubes are fabric structures made from interlaced fibers, offering high strength and durability, making them suitable for applications requiring filtration and support in sediment control. In contrast, nonwoven geotextile tubes consist of randomly oriented fibers, providing excellent permeability and drainage, ideal for quicker dewatering processes. Both types are utilized in environmental projects, construction, and waste management for effective sediment handling and water removal.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>LEC Grown InP</li><li>VGF Grown InP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The InP wafer market is primarily segmented into two types: LEC (Low-Pressure Vertical Bridgman) grown InP and VGF (Vertical Gradient Freeze) grown InP. LEC grown InP wafers are known for their high purity and uniformity, making them suitable for advanced optoelectronic applications. In contrast, VGF grown InP wafers offer superior thermal stability and scalability, appealing for high-volume production. Both types serve critical roles in the semiconductor industry, particularly in photonics and telecommunications technologies.</p></p>

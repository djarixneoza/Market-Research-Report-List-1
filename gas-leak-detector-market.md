<p><p>The gas leak detector market comprises several types tailored for specific applications. Electrochemical detectors monitor gas concentrations through chemical reactions, offering high sensitivity and selectivity. Semiconductor detectors utilize sensor materials that change resistance in the presence of gas, providing cost-effective solutions. Ultrasonic detectors identify gas leaks by detecting sound waves generated during gas escape. Infrared detectors use light absorption methods to detect specific gases at various concentrations. Additionally, other technologies may encompass photoionization and flame ionization methods, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>

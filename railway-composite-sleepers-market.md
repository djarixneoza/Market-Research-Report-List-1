<p><p>The railway composite sleepers market is classified into three main types: ordinary sleepers, turnout sleepers, and bridge sleepers. Ordinary sleepers are the standard support structures used along rail tracks, providing stability and alignment. Turnout sleepers facilitate track switches, allowing trains to change paths safely. Bridge sleepers are specifically designed for bridges, ensuring structural integrity over spans. Each type serves distinct functions, contributing to the overall safety, durability, and efficiency of railway infrastructure.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Venting Type</li><li>Closed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IV infusion set market comprises two primary types: venting and closed systems. Venting type sets allow air to enter the IV bag, ensuring a continuous fluid flow, which can accommodate various medication delivery needs. In contrast, closed type sets prevent air from entering, reducing contamination risks and maintaining a sterile environment. Both types are essential in clinical settings, offering different advantages regarding safety and fluid management, catering to specific patient requirements and healthcare protocols.</p></p>

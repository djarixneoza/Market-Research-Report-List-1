<p><ul><li>Evaporative Coolers</li><li>Dry Air Cooler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fluid coolers market is segmented into evaporative coolers and dry air coolers. Evaporative coolers utilize water evaporation to absorb heat, providing energy-efficient cooling suitable for dry climates. They operate by drawing warm air through wet pads, lowering the air temperature. In contrast, dry air coolers rely on air-to-air heat exchange without moisture, making them ideal for fluid cooling in environments with strict humidity controls. Both systems offer unique advantages for various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>

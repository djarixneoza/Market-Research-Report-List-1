<p><p>The leather furniture market is categorized into three main types: faux leather, half leather, and whole leather. Faux leather furniture offers an affordable and eco-friendly option, mimicking the look of real leather without the use of animal products. Half leather furniture features leather on key touchpoints, combined with synthetic materials for a balanced price and durability. Whole leather furniture, crafted entirely from genuine leather, provides luxury and longevity, appealing to consumers seeking high-quality, premium products. Each type caters to different budgets and preferences.</p></p>

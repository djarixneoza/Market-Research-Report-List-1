<p><ul><li>Inner DC Blocks</li><li>Inner-outer DC Blocks</li><li>Outer DC Blocks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DC block market comprises three main types: Inner DC Blocks, Inner-Outer DC Blocks, and Outer DC Blocks. Inner DC Blocks are designed to filter direct current from RF signals within internal circuitry, ensuring signal integrity. Inner-Outer DC Blocks serve dual purposes, providing isolation for both internal components and external connections, enhancing versatility. Outer DC Blocks primarily focus on protecting external components from DC offsets while allowing AC signals to pass, safeguarding sensitive devices from potential damage and interference.</p></p>

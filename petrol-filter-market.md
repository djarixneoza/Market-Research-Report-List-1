<p><strong>The Petrol Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Carburetor</li><li>EFI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The petrol filter market is segmented primarily into two categories based on fuel delivery systems: carburetor and Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI). Carburetor filters are typically designed for older vehicles that use a mechanical system for fuel mixing, focusing on debris removal to ensure proper airflow and fuel atomization. In contrast, EFI filters cater to modern vehicles with advanced fuel management, providing higher precision filtration to maintain fuel pressure and prevent clogging in sophisticated injectors. Both types are essential for engine performance and longevity.</p></p>

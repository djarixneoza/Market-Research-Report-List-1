<p><p>The Steering Auxiliary Lamp market primarily consists of two types: Xenon and LED. Xenon lamps, known for their bright, white light, enhance visibility during night driving and harsh weather conditions. They utilize a gas discharge principle, providing a powerful beam. In contrast, LED lamps offer energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and quick response times. They produce bright light while consuming less power, making them popular for modern vehicles. Both types improve driving safety by illuminating curves and enhancing road visibility during turns.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Negative Pressure Type</li><li>Positive Pressure Type</li><li>Robotic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic pool cleaners market comprises three main types: Negative Pressure, Positive Pressure, and Robotic cleaners. Negative Pressure cleaners rely on suction to remove debris, using the pool's filtration system. Positive Pressure cleaners, on the other hand, use an integrated pump to propel water and waste toward a collection bag. Robotic cleaners operate independently with their own power source, employing advanced navigation and cleaning features. Each type offers unique benefits, catering to various pool maintenance needs and preferences.</p></p>

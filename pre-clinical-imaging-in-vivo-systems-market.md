<p><p>The pre-clinical imaging (in-vivo) systems market encompasses various technologies that facilitate detailed visualization of biological processes in living organisms. Optical imaging systems utilize light to capture images at high resolution. Nuclear imaging systems rely on radioactive tracers for internal visualization. Micro-MRI offers high-resolution imaging of small specimens. Micro-ultrasound provides real-time imaging using sound waves. Photoacoustic imaging combines light and ultrasound for tissue analysis. Magnetic particle imaging utilizes magnetic nanoparticles for precise imaging, while "others" includes emerging technologies and hybrid systems.</p></p>

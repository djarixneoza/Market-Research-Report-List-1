<p><p>The Demagnetization Tunnels market consists of two primary types: High-Frequency Demagnetization Tunnels and Low-Frequency Demagnetization Tunnels. High-Frequency Demagnetization Tunnels utilize rapid alternating magnetic fields to efficiently demagnetize ferromagnetic materials, leading to quicker processing times. In contrast, Low-Frequency Demagnetization Tunnels operate at slower frequencies, providing a more gradual demagnetization process, which can be beneficial for delicate components. Both types serve various industrial applications, ensuring materials are restored to their non-magnetized states for optimal functionality.</p></p>

<p><strong>The FRD Single Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Common Yang</li><li>Common Cathode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>FRD (Flyback Rectifier Diode) single tube market includes two main types: Common Yang and Common Cathode. The Common Yang type features a configuration where the anodes of multiple diodes are connected to a common point, suitable for high voltage applications. In contrast, the Common Cathode type has the cathodes linked together, effectively managing current flow and improving thermal stability. Both types cater to distinct electronic needs, ensuring efficiency in power management and rectification across various electronic devices.</p></p>

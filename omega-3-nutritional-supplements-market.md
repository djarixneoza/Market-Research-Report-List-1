<p><p>The Omega-3 nutritional supplements market comprises several types. Fish oil is the most common source, rich in EPA and DHA, promoting heart health. Krill oil, derived from tiny crustaceans, offers similar benefits with additional antioxidants. Mammalian oil, less common, comes from seal or whale sources, and is known for high omega-3 content. Algal oil, a plant-based alternative, is popular among vegetarians and vegans, delivering omega-3 without animal products. Other types may include blended oils or fortified products with varying omega-3 sources.</p></p>

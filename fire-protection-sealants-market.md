<p><strong>The Fire Protection Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Elastometric Type Sealants</li><li>Intumescent Type Sealants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire protection sealants market comprises primarily two types: elastomeric and intumescent sealants. Elastomeric sealants are flexible materials that can withstand movement and stress, making them ideal for applications where joint movement is expected. Intumescent sealants, on the other hand, expand upon exposure to heat, creating an insulating barrier that prevents fire and smoke from spreading through penetrations and joints. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing fire safety in various construction and remodeling projects.</p></p>

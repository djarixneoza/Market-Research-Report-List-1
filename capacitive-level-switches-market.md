<p><strong>The Capacitive Level Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dielectric Type (D-Type)</li><li>Conductive Type (L-Type)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The capacitive level switches market comprises two main types: Dielectric Type (D-Type) and Conductive Type (L-Type). D-Type switches measure the dielectric constant of materials, making them suitable for non-conductive liquids and solid applications. They are ideal for detecting levels in tanks or silos. In contrast, L-Type switches utilize the conductivity of materials, excelling in conductive liquids like water or slurries. Both types offer reliable level detection but cater to different applications based on the material properties involved.</p></p>

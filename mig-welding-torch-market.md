<p><p>MIG welding torches are available in two primary types: water-cooled and air-cooled. Water-cooled torches use a cooling system to circulate water around the torch, making them suitable for prolonged welding tasks due to their ability to dissipate heat effectively. In contrast, air-cooled torches rely on ambient air for cooling, making them more portable and easier to manage for shorter projects. Both types cater to different welding applications, balancing performance and convenience based on user needs.</p></p>

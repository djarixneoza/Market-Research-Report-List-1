<p><ul><li>Absorbable Membrane</li><li>Non-absorbable Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental guided tissue regeneration membrane market comprises two main types: absorbable and non-absorbable membranes. Absorbable membranes dissolve over time, eliminating the need for a surgical removal and promoting tissue regeneration while minimizing patient discomfort. Non-absorbable membranes, on the other hand, provide lasting support but require a secondary procedure for removal. Both types play crucial roles in periodontal therapies, enhancing the healing process, and guiding tissue growth to restore lost or compromised dental structures effectively.</p></p>

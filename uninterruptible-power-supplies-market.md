<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Uninterruptible Power Supplies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Off-line or standby</li><li>Line-interactive</li><li>Online or double-conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS) come in three main types: </p><p>1. **Off-line or Standby UPS**: This type provides basic battery backup, activating only when power fails, offering essential protection for equipment. </p><p>2. **Line-interactive UPS**: This variant maintains voltage levels by adjusting incoming power, providing better surge protection and battery support without switching to battery mode as often.</p><p>3. **Online or Double-Conversion UPS**: It continuously converts incoming AC power to DC and back to AC, ensuring clean, uninterrupted power supply, ideal for sensitive devices needing constant voltage and frequency stability.</p></p>
